About Us

Founded in 2021, Aditi Traders is one of the fastest growing engineering organizations in Karnataka. Based in Belagavi, we specialize in delivering engineering and automation solutions tailored for industries across India.

With a clear vision to provide top-quality services and components to the engineering and manufacturing sectors, we have grown consistently and now aim for a turnover of ₹6.5 Crores in FY 2023–2024. Our organization is completely debt-free and built upon strong technical foundations.

We are known for:

  • Highly qualified and trained professionals
  • Strong domain expertise in trading, automation, and control systems
  • Reliable and timely project execution
  • Partnerships with leading global manufacturers

With over 5–6 years of experience in the engineering and industrial sector, we continue to lead with innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.
